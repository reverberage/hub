## What It Is

- European Commission-funded grant program for FOSS, open standards, open hardware
- Led by NLnet Foundation (public benefit org)
- e50M+ granted to hundreds of projects
- Open to: individuals, research orgs, non-profits, companies of any size

---

## Active Program: NGI Restack

- **Grants**: e5,000 to e50,000
- **Goal**: "Building a healthy open internet stack"
- **Calls**: Rolling open calls, deadline every 2 months
- **Next deadline**: August 1, 2026
- **Eligibility**: All FOSS projects. Anyone can apply.
