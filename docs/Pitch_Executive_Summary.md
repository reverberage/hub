# Pitch Deck Executive Summary

**Journalism doesn't need another CMS; it needs a Studio.**

lo6 is the first "Newsroom Operating System" designed as a creative instrument—an "Ableton Live for News"—that orchestrates AI agents to handle the drudgery of sourcing and drafting while keeping the journalist in total creative control. Unlike "black box" generators that hallucinate, our **Human-in-the-Loop** architecture enforces strict provenance, visually linking every claim to a verified source to solve the trust crisis in modern media. Built on enterprise-grade security (OWASP/NIST), we offer the speed of automation with the integrity of traditional reporting. This is not just a tool for managing content; it is an exoskeleton for the investigative mind.
