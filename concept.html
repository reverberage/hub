AI-generated content is drowning local newsrooms — and they don't have the tools to fight back

Reuters Institute for the Study of Journalism · June 2, 2026

A new survey of 200 local newsrooms across Europe and North America reveals a stark reality: 68% of editors report spending more time verifying AI-generated content than producing original reporting. The tools they use — basic reverse image search, manual source tracing — were designed for a pre-generative-AI era.

"We're fighting machines with spreadsheets," said one editor at a mid-size German regional paper. "The AI slop arrives faster than we can debunk it."
